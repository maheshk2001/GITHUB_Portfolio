Schematic and Layout Design of a 4-bit Adder

Arizona State University
Nov 2023 - Dec 2023
Tempe, Arizona
Cadence Virtuoso Layout Design Schematic Design VLSI Design

Project Overview

This project involved designing the schematic of a 1-bit mirror adder with appropriate sizing for the transistors based on transition time requirements. The design focused on creating an optimal floor plan considering area, delay, and power constraints, with routing optimization to minimize these constraints.

The 1-bit adder was instantiated four times to build a complete 4-bit adder, demonstrating modular design principles and efficient layout techniques in VLSI design.

Key Achievements

  • Designed schematic of 1-bit mirror adder with appropriate transistor sizing
  • Optimized transistor sizing based on transition time requirements
  • Created floor plan for design keeping area, delay and power constraints in mind
  • Performed routing to minimize given constraints
  • Instantiated 1-bit adder four times to build 4-bit adder
  • Demonstrated modular design principles in VLSI layout
